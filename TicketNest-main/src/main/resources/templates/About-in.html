About Us

Welcome to TicketNest

TicketNest is an online ticket booking system designed to provide a seamless and efficient booking experience for various travel and entertainment services. Our platform allows users to book train tickets, bus tickets, and movie tickets with ease, ensuring convenience and reliability.

Our Mission

At TicketNest, our mission is to simplify the ticket booking process by offering a user-friendly interface, secure transactions, and real-time availability updates. We strive to make travel and entertainment bookings hassle-free for our users.

Features of TicketNest

  • Multi-Service Booking: Book train, bus, and movie tickets in one place.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive design for a seamless experience.
  • Real-Time Updates: Get instant confirmation and availability status.
  • Secure Transactions: Safe and encrypted payment options.

Technologies Used

Backend: Java, Spring Boot, JDBC

Frontend: HTML, CSS

Database: MySQL.

Future Enhancements

We aim to expand TicketNest by incorporating additional features such as:

  • Admin features, allows the admin to effortlessly add or delete new Buses, Trains and Movies
  • AI-Based Suggestions, Implement AI to recommend relevant movies or travel routes based on user preferences and past booking history.
  • E-Ticket Generation, Enable automatic generation of e-tickets in PDF format, which can be downloaded or sent to the user via email after successful booking.
  • Multiple Ticket Integration : Allow users to book multiple tickets (e.g., bus + movie or train + movie) in a single session and complete the payment in one transaction for convenience
